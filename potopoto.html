Novel : Une enfant de Poto-Poto


This novel tells the story of Kimia, from the festivities of "Dipanda" in 1960, to the expansion of mobile phones into everyday life. The novel covers more than four decades, offering the reader a good slice of Congo's political history and the evolution of Congolese society, as the narrator retraces her journey, her studies with her friend Pélagie, their fascination with one of their teachers, M. Franceschini, who arrived from France to teach her. Kimia's story continues with her obtaining a scholarship to the United States, while Pélagie obtains one to France, her career as a novelist, their respective marriages, and the permanent return to her native country.

Author : Henri Lopes


Henri LOPES est né en 1937 à Kinshasa (Congo). Il est diplomate, écrivain et homme politique. Premier ministre de la République Populaire du Congo (1972-1975) et auteur de la parole de l’hymne national du Congo (Les trois glorieuses) usité de 1971 à 1991. Tribaliques est le premier fruit de sa plume. Il est l’auteur d’autres œuvres –romans, récits- comme : Sans tam-tam, Pleurer-rire, Sur l’autre rive, le Chercheur d’Afriques.
