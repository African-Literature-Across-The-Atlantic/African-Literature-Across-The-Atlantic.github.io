Novel : Fresh Water


Ada, whose name means “the egg of a python,” is born in Umuahia, Nigeria, to Saachi and Saul. She carries within her ogbanje—spirit children of the gods—who often die young, but Ada survives. From an early age, she shows signs of instability: she slithers instead of crawling and is prone to violent outbursts. At age three, after a disturbing encounter with a python, her father kills the snake, unintentionally making a sacrifice that keeps her body alive but fractures her mind. As she grows, Ada is haunted by the ogbanje, who awaken fully during a New Year masquerade. Realizing they are immortal gods trapped in a human body, they rebel against their confinement.


Author : Akwaeke Emezi


AKWAEKE is a Nigerian-born, multidisciplinary artist working across literature, music, film, and visual art. They broke out with their 2018 debut novel Freshwater and have since published eight books, including The Death of Vivek Oji and Pet, a National Book Award finalist. Their 2023 romance You Made a Fool of Death With Your Beauty was an NAACP Image Award nominee and sold to Amazon Studios in a seven-figure deal. Based in Brooklyn, AKWAEKE’s debut EP Stop Dying, You Were Very Expensive! dropped in 2024, alongside their latest novel Little Rot.
